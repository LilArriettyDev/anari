ANARI V0.0.1

Meet AnAri, the robot car that combines the names of its creators, Anit and Arrietty.

Yes, I am Arrietty, and my younger brother is Anit.
AnAri is a primary robot car originally developed using Arduino technology, but we have big plans for its future.
AnAri may have started as a simple robot car, but we are determined to turn it into a modern, state-of-the-art machine incorporating all the latest technologies. With a focus on efficiency, intelligence, and ease of use, AnAri is poised to become a powerful tool for a wide range of applications.

The Evolution of AnAri: Present and Future Features

Multi-Platform Control

Arduino for basic controls, ESP32 for wireless capabilities, and Raspberry Pi for high-level computations.

Air Quality Monitoring

Includes sensors to monitor the environmental conditions.

Wi-Fi and Bluetooth Connectivity

Offers remote operation capabilities through Wi-Fi and Bluetooth technologies.

Basic Navigation

Capable of forward, backward, and turning movements for general navigation.

Advanced Obstacle Avoidance

Employs ultrasonic sensors to detect and navigate around obstacles in real time.

Real-Time Sensor Data Streaming

Leverage the ESP32's high data throughput to stream sensor data in real-time to a remote interface for monitoring.

Battery Monitoring

Use the ESP32's ADC (Analog to Digital Converter) to monitor battery voltage and alert when it's time to recharge.
