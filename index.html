I'm Arrietty Islam.

Eager to Shape the 4th Industrial Revolution

Get to Know Me

Hello there! I'm a vibrant first-grader at Monipur High School & College in Dhaka with a big passion for all things robotic. I believe robots are more than just cool—they're the future, and I'm excited to be part of shaping it.
But don't be fooled, I'm not just about robotics! I'm an explorer at heart and love discovering new places and experiences. Martial arts keep me fit, focused, and disciplined. And when it's time for a bit of calm, I dive into the wonderful world of quilling, turning simple paper into intricate works of art.
My mind isn't only for creativity; I also have a knack for mental math, honed through my work with tools like the abacus. Plus, I enjoy getting a breath of fresh air while pedaling away on my bicycle, exploring the neighborhood.
I have big dreams. One day, I hope to design robots that will transform lives globally. I'm not just dreaming about the future—I'm actively working towards it. I welcome you to explore my website and delve into the projects that I'm working on. Thank you for stopping by!
